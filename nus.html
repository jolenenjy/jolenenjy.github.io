NUS DID Gradshow 16

For the NUS Division of Industrial Design Grad Show 2016, I designed and developed the showcase website for the graduating batch.

I took the opportunity to learn more apout front end development and gained a better appreciation for responsive web dev.

The site is intended to be fairly straightforward, it consists of an overview of the graduating batch on the home page and personal project pages. Wanting to experiment a little more, I explored introducing some moving elements.

Eventually, I settled on something more subtle by simply alternating the colour of the header text using the event brand colors.

Reflection

Given the limited time I had to work on this, there are still many aspects I would like to improve. Aside from some minor spacing adjustments, on hindsight, I would have liked to spend more time focusing on the performance of the site. Image optimisation and minfiying are definitely some of the things I overlooked.

However, I am grateful for the learning opportunity and now pay keen attention both when delivering my design assets and writing prototype code.
