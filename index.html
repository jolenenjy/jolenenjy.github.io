  • Meter

    1st Runner Up, Mobile Application Design for Smart Metering Call-For-Proposal
    Invision Prototype




    Brief
    To provide timely and useful information feedback to consumers on their utility consumption (electricity, town gas and water) via their smartphones.


    Research
    With only the brief in hand, my partner and I identified two key questions we needed to have answered before we could start designing:

    What utility information are people interested in seeing and why?

    In what order of importance should the peices of information be displayed?

    Realising this was not a question we could answer ourselves, we sat down with 30 people to ask them about their utility and conservation habits. We approached people from first-time house owners to singles who lived alone to long-married couples, all staying in a wide variety of housing types, and synthesised our findings to inform our design.

    Grouping of information to form the app main pages
