About me

Hello again! My name is Jolene Ng.

I'm a UX designer at GovTech, shaping the way citizens interact with the Singapore Government. Currently, I work on the Parents Gateway mobile and web app, improving communications between parents and public schools.

Through working with various teams, I discovered that nothing makes me happier than working with a team who believes in their product; and the value it creates for people.
