UX Snapshots

Here lies a collection of my previous ux projects and a brief summary of my role and tools used for each of them.

NUS DID Gradshow event site

For the NUS DID Grad show 2016, a showcase website for the graduating batch was required. While there was the option to use an existing website builder, I decided to take on the task of developing it.

You may visit the site here.


My Role

I started designing and developing the website after a night of tutorials. Taking the opportunity to learn more about front-end development, I gained a better appreciation for responsive web dev and design.


Tools

Sketch, HTML/CSS/JS

VisaRing

VISA Design Storm is a 4-day workshop that seeks to envision VISA payment of the 21st century. It intends to inform and influence how people everywhere in the world will conduct VISA payments and how it will function as a bridge to evolving digital world.

As part of a four man team, we worked a future concept for visa payments.


My Role

I was primarily in charge of ideation and storytelling. Other than pitching our idea, I also worked alongside a teammate on the prototyping.


Tools

Origami, After Effects, 3D printing


This or That

In collaboration with Essec Business School

This or that is a product discovery app that allows users to browse for products online without being overwhelmed by options. By reinventing the shopping experience as a comparison game, we can both collect the user's preferences while allowing them to browse.


My Role

Together with another teammate, we lead the project from inception to iterations. I took charge of pitching, prototyping the iteractions and assisted with the UI deisgn.


Tools

Origami, After Effects, Pixate
